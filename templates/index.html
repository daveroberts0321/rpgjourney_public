The Journey

You are the Hero of your own story. You will be the one to set the tasks, we just help you reach your milestones.

The Journey is about realizing where you are and setting your feet on a path of improvement. Each day you will be bettering yourself not to reach any goal but to become the person you are capable of being. Our task are only focused on small improvements at first but as you grow those improvements will become larger and more impactful as the milestones compound. The more you advance, so do the milestones.

Do not be fooled into thinking this will be easy. You will be starting at level zero and the first 20 milestones will be the easiest to reach. At level 1 you will have already completed 20 milestones in any aspect but now the real work begins. The weights will be getting heavier, the runs faster, the savings larger, and the income requirements more difficult. Study times will require more time. Some might stretch over a few days or weeks. The key is to keep moving forward.

This simple example shows the power of exponential growth. The first 20 milestones will be the easiest to reach but as you grow the milestones will become more difficult, but the next step will always just be an attainable 1% improvement over yesterday. Once a goal is reached, simply end the goal, start a new one, and keep your rank progress...

If you have an emergency, or have to use your savings for example... no problem, just set your new baseline and the process picks up where you left off. No lost progress, no lost time, just a new starting point.
